Playstyle

The Sorcadin is the undisputed king of single-target nova damage. Use Sorcerer Metamagic (Quickened Spell) to cast a leveled spell as a bonus action, then follow up with weapon attacks fueled by Divine Smite. Twinned Haste on yourself and a martial ally doubles the pain. You dictate when fights end by dumping every spell slot into Smites on the turn that matters most.

Why It's Powerful
  • Divine Smite converts any spell slot into burst melee damage — Sorcerer slots included
  • Metamagic (Quickened + Twinned) lets you Haste two allies or blast + Smite in the same turn
  • Half-Orc Savage Attacks adds an extra weapon die on crits, stacking with Smite
  • Extra Attack at Paladin 5 plus Haste attack = 3 Smite-eligible strikes per turn
  • Aura of Protection at Paladin 6 gives +CHA to all saves for the whole party
Key Feats
  • Great Weapon Master — bonus action attack on kill/crit, +10 damage at -5 to hit (offset by Vow of Enmity advantage)
  • Savage Attacker — roll weapon damage twice, take higher. Smooths out Smite variance
Essential Spells & Abilities
  • Vow of Enmity — advantage on all attacks vs one target (negates GWM penalty)
  • Divine Smite — 2d8+ radiant damage per slot level on hit
  • Haste — extra action, +2 AC, doubled movement; Twin it on an ally
  • Shield — +5 AC reaction, keeps you alive in melee
  • Quickened Spell — cast a full spell as a bonus action
  • Twinned Spell — target two creatures with single-target spells
Recommended Gear
  • Balduran's Giantslayer — doubles STR modifier on damage, advantage vs large+ creatures
  • Helldusk Armour — heavy armour with no STR requirement, fire immunity, Fly
  • Amulet of Greater Health — sets CON to 23, massive HP boost
  • Gauntlets of Hill Giant Strength — sets STR to 23 (makes dump-stat STR viable)

Playstyle

Start every fight from stealth to trigger Assassin's auto-crit on surprised enemies. Dread Ambusher grants a free extra attack with bonus damage on turn 1. Combined with Action Surge, you can unleash 6+ attacks before the enemy takes a single action. After your explosive opener, use Cunning Action to Hide and repeat the process.

Why It's Powerful
  • Assassin's Assassinate guarantees critical hits against surprised targets
  • Dread Ambusher adds a free attack + 1d8 damage on the first round
  • Action Surge doubles your action economy for a devastating opener
  • Sneak Attack adds massive bonus damage once per turn
  • Duergar Invisibility provides reliable stealth without spell slots
  • 6+ attacks on turn 1 — most fights end before the enemy acts
Key Feats
  • Sharpshooter — +10 ranged damage at -5 to hit (auto-crits offset the penalty)
  • +2 DEX — reach 20 DEX for maximum attack/damage bonus
Essential Spells & Abilities
  • Dread Ambusher — bonus attack + 1d8 + enhanced initiative on round 1
  • Assassinate — auto-crit surprised enemies, advantage vs those who haven't acted
  • Sneak Attack — 2d6 bonus damage (advantage or ally adjacent)
  • Action Surge — second full action (Fighter 2)
  • Pass Without Trace — +10 Stealth for the whole party
  • Cunning Action: Hide — bonus action to re-stealth mid-combat
Recommended Gear
  • Titanstring Bow — adds STR modifier to damage (combine with Gauntlets of Hill Giant Strength)
  • Shadow of Menzoberranzan — grants Shroud of Shadow (free invisibility once/short rest)
  • Gloves of Archery — +2 to ranged weapon damage rolls
  • Cloak of Displacement — disadvantage on attacks against you until hit

Playstyle

The Lockadin solves the Paladin's biggest weakness: running out of Smite slots. Warlock Pact Magic slots recharge on short rest, giving you a reliable stream of level-3 Smites all day long. Deepened Pact (Warlock 5) grants a third attack via Pact of the Blade. Oathbreaker's Aura of Hate adds CHA to melee damage for you and undead allies — including summoned ones.

Why It's Powerful
  • Short-rest Pact Magic slots mean you never run dry on Smites
  • Deepened Pact = 3 attacks per turn (more Smite opportunities than Sorcadin)
  • Oathbreaker Aura of Hate adds +CHA to all melee damage
  • Dark One's Blessing heals you on every kill (Fiend Warlock)
  • Aura of Protection at Paladin 6 still protects the whole party
Key Feats
  • Great Weapon Master — +10 damage, bonus action attack on kill/crit
  • +2 CHA — boosts Smite DC, Aura of Protection, and Aura of Hate
Essential Spells & Abilities
  • Divine Smite — fueled by short-rest Pact slots
  • Aura of Hate — +CHA melee damage to self and nearby undead/fiends
  • Aura of Protection — +CHA to all saving throws within 10ft
  • Darkness + Devil's Sight — you see, enemies don't; permanent advantage
  • Eldritch Blast — ranged fallback with Agonizing Blast (+CHA damage)
  • Animate Dead — raise skeletons that benefit from Aura of Hate
Recommended Gear
  • Balduran's Giantslayer — doubles STR mod, advantage on large+ enemies
  • Helldusk Armour — fire immunity, Fly, no STR requirement
  • Helmet of Arcane Acuity — stacking Arcane Acuity on hit for spell DCs
  • Ring of Free Action — immune to paralysis, difficult terrain

Playstyle

Throw everything that isn't nailed down — and some things that are. Tavern Brawler doubles your STR modifier on thrown attacks, and Berserker Frenzy + Thief's extra bonus action means you can throw 4+ times per turn. You have the range safety of an archer with the raw damage scaling of a melee bruiser. Works from level 4 onwards and only gets more absurd.

Why It's Powerful
  • Tavern Brawler adds STR modifier TWICE to thrown attack and damage rolls
  • Berserker's Frenzied Throw = bonus action throw while raging
  • Thief's extra bonus action = two bonus action throws per turn
  • Rage damage applies to thrown weapons (STR-based)
  • Doesn't require spell slots, concentration, or setup — just throw and win
Key Feats
  • Tavern Brawler — the cornerstone. Doubles STR mod on throws, +1 STR
  • +2 STR — every point of STR is doubled by Tavern Brawler
Essential Spells & Abilities
  • Frenzy — Frenzied Throw/Strike as bonus action while raging
  • Fast Hands — second bonus action per turn (Thief 3)
  • Rage — bonus damage, resistance to physical damage
  • Extra Attack — two throws per action
  • Action Surge — four throws in one round (Fighter 2)
Recommended Gear
  • Nyrulna — legendary trident, returns when thrown, deals 3d6 thunder AoE on impact
  • Ring of Flinging — +1d4 damage on thrown attacks
  • Gauntlets of Hill Giant Strength — STR 23, doubled by Tavern Brawler
  • Enraging Heart Garb — Wrath on each attack while raging (+1 damage per stack)

Playstyle

Stack Arcane Acuity via Scorching Ray (each ray counts as a hit) to push your spell DC into the 20s, then unleash a Heightened Hold Monster or devastating AoE. Hat of Fire Acuity is the engine — every fire hit grants +1 to spell attack and DC. With 20 CHA, Elemental Adept bypassing resistance, and Draconic bonus damage on every fire spell, nothing survives.

Why It's Powerful
  • Hat of Fire Acuity stacks Arcane Acuity on each Scorching Ray hit (3–6 stacks per cast)
  • Spell DCs reach 22+ after one Scorching Ray, making save-or-suck spells near-guaranteed
  • Draconic Ancestry adds CHA mod to all fire spell damage
  • Elemental Adept (Fire) ignores fire resistance and rerolls 1s on fire damage dice
  • Single-class = level 6 spells (Disintegrate, Chain Lightning, Globe of Invulnerability)
  • Halfling Lucky rerolls natural 1s on attacks/saves
Key Feats
  • +2 CHA — reach 20 CHA for best DCs and Draconic bonus
  • Elemental Adept (Fire) — ignore fire resistance, reroll 1s on fire damage
  • Alert — +5 initiative, never surprised. Act first to remove threats
Essential Spells & Abilities
  • Scorching Ray — 3 fire rays, each stacks Arcane Acuity
  • Fireball — 8d6 AoE, boosted by Draconic Ancestry
  • Haste — extra action for more Scorching Rays or movement
  • Hold Monster — Heighten after Acuity stacks = auto-success
  • Disintegrate — 10d6+40 single target nuke
  • Shield — +5 AC reaction for survivability
  • Quickened Spell — bonus action Scorching Ray to stack Acuity, then action for the big spell
Recommended Gear
  • Markoheshkir — legendary quarterstaff, +1 spell DC, free spell per long rest, elemental attunement
  • Hat of Fire Acuity — Arcane Acuity on fire damage (the build's cornerstone)
  • Potent Robe — +CHA to cantrip damage, temp HP at start of each turn
  • Birthright — +2 CHA (stacks beyond 20)

Playstyle

Summon Shadow Blade for a high-damage CHA-based weapon that deals psychic damage (rarely resisted), then swing it three times per action with Fighter 11's third Extra Attack. Action Surge doubles the output to six Shadow Blade attacks in a turn. Hexblade's Curse adds proficiency bonus to every damage roll, and Hex's Curse heals you on kills.

Why It's Powerful
  • Three attacks per action at Fighter 11 — six with Action Surge
  • Shadow Blade deals psychic damage (almost nothing resists it)
  • Hexblade lets you attack with CHA instead of STR/DEX

  • Heavy armour + Shield spell + Helldusk = extremely tanky

Playstyle

Cast Armour of Agathys for massive temp HP, then hide it behind the Abjuration Ward which absorbs all incoming damage first. Enemies who hit you take cold revenge damage from Agathys — but the temp HP never drops because the Ward tanks the hit. Combine with Shield, Mirror Image, and Counterspell to become literally unkillable while passively dealing damage to anyone who touches you.

Why It's Powerful
  • Arcane Ward absorbs damage BEFORE Armour of Agathys temp HP is touched
  • Agathys revenge damage stays active indefinitely because the temp HP never drops
  • Every Abjuration spell cast recharges the Ward (Shield, Counterspell, etc.)
  • Draconic (White) adds CHA mod to cold damage spells
  • Tempest Cleric's Destructive Wrath maximizes lightning/thunder damage once per short rest
  • Still a full Wizard with access to the entire spell list
Key Feats
  • +2 INT (x2) — reach 20 INT for best spell DCs and Ward HP
Essential Spells & Abilities
  • Armour of Agathys — temp HP + cold revenge damage to melee attackers
  • Arcane Ward — absorbs damage, recharged by casting Abjuration spells
  • Shield — +5 AC AND recharges Arcane Ward
  • Counterspell — deny enemy casters AND recharge Ward
  • Mirror Image — three decoys that absorb attacks
  • Destructive Wrath — maximize one lightning/thunder damage roll per short rest
  • Globe of Invulnerability — immunity to all spells level 5 and below
Recommended Gear
  • Mourning Frost — +1 cold damage per hit, Frostbite on target
  • Robe of the Weave — +1 spell DC, +1 spell attack, AC bonus
  • Coldbrim Hat — apply Encrusted with Frost on cold damage
  • Boots of Persistence — Freedom of Movement, Longstrider

Playstyle

A whirlwind of fists. Flurry of Blows gives two bonus action punches, and Thief's Fast Hands gives you a second bonus action for another Flurry — that's 6 unarmed strikes per turn. Open Hand techniques add push, prone, or no-reaction effects to every Flurry. At Monk 9, Quivering Palm lets you instantly kill any target that fails a CON save. The ultimate boss assassin.

Why It's Powerful
  • 6 attacks per turn (2 base + 2 Flurry + 2 from extra bonus action)
  • Quivering Palm = instant kill on failed CON save (Monk 9)
  • Stunning Strike on any hit forces a CON save or the target loses their turn
  • Wood Elf's extra movement + Monk speed = 18m+ per turn
  • No gear dependency — Gloves of Soul Catching in Act 3 turns you into a god
  • Open Hand Flurry effects: push, knock prone, prevent reactions (no save)
Key Feats
  • Tavern Brawler — +1 STR, double STR mod on unarmed (changes attack stat to STR)
  • +2 WIS — boosts AC (unarmoured), Ki DC, and Stunning Strike DC
Essential Spells & Abilities
  • Flurry of Blows — 2 bonus action unarmed strikes (1 Ki)
  • Fast Hands — second bonus action for another Flurry (Thief 3)
  • Stunning Strike — stun on hit (CON save)
  • Quivering Palm — instant kill or 10d10 damage on CON fail (Monk 9)
  • Step of the Wind — bonus action Dash or Disengage
  • Deflect Missiles — catch and redirect ranged attacks
Recommended Gear
  • Gloves of Soul Catching — +1d10 force per unarmed hit, heal or get Advantage
  • Haste Helm — free Haste for 3 turns at combat start (extra action = more punches)
  • Cloak of Displacement — disadvantage on attacks against you
  • Boots of Uninhibited Kushigo — +WIS mod to unarmed damage

Playstyle

Patch 8 — New Subclasses

Patch 8 introduces several new subclasses that will shake up the meta. Here's what's coming and how it affects the tier list:

Hexblade (Warlock)

CHA-based melee attacks. Eliminates the MAD (multi-ability dependent) problem for Warlock dips. The Eldritch Hexknight build (#6) becomes even stronger — possibly jumps to top 5. Every CHA-based multiclass benefits.

Shadow Magic (Sorcerer)

Darkness synergy + Strength of the Grave (cheat death). Pairs naturally with Devil's Sight Warlock dips. Expect a new "Shadow Sorcadin" variant to emerge as a Sorcadin alternative with built-in Darkness combo.

Swashbuckler (Rogue)

CHA-based initiative + easy Sneak Attack without needing advantage or an adjacent ally. Makes melee Rogue builds viable. Astarion finally has a compelling melee option that doesn't feel inferior to ranged.

Path of the Giant (Barbarian)

Enlarged size + reach + thrown weapon bonuses. Purpose-built for the Throwzerker archetype. Could push build #4 even higher if the thrown weapon scaling stacks with Tavern Brawler.

Circle of Stars (Druid)

Starry Form grants bonus action healing, archer damage, or concentration advantage. A strong alternative to Moon Druid for players who prefer casting over Wild Shape. Competitive with Light Cleric for the support slot.

Bladesinging (Wizard)

Extra Attack with a cantrip replacement + INT to AC and concentration saves. Makes Wizard a legitimate melee-caster. The Arcane Tank build (#8) may shift to Bladesinger for more offensive capability while maintaining survivability.
