Pure Moon Druid

Forest Gnome · Circle of the Moon · Druid 12
Tank Shapeshifter Full Caster Patch 7+

Overview

Pure Moon Druid is the strongest Wild Shape build in BG3. You get 3 attacks per turn at level 10, elemental Myrmidon forms with massive AoE, the highest Wild Shape HP, and full 6th-level spellcasting for when you're not shifted. No multiclass can match it.

Why Not Multiclass?

Most martial features (Rage damage, Reckless Attack, Flurry of Blows, Stunning Strike) do NOT work in Wild Shape beast forms. Barbarian only gives resistance — no damage bonus. Monk is a trap. Pure Druid gives you Improved Wild Strike (3 attacks), Myrmidon forms, and better Wild Shape HP. Nothing beats it.

Race — Forest Gnome

  • Racial Gnome Cunning — Advantage on INT/WIS/CHA saves vs spells. One of the best passives in the game.
  • Racial Speak with Animals — Free once per long rest. Saves a prepared spell.
  • Racial Darkvision — 12m range.

Two Characters in One

In beast form you're a frontline tank with 2–3 attacks. In gnome form you're a full caster with 6th level spells. Shift → tank → revert → cast → shift again. You never run out of things to do.

Combat Guide

Standard Rotation

  1. Pre-buff Before Combat
    Longstrider on everyone (lasts all day). Heroes' Feast before boss fights. Plant Growth on chokepoints.
  2. Opening spell or shift Turn 1
    Option A: Cast a big spell (Call Lightning, Entangle) then shift next turn.
    Option B: Wild Shape immediately (bonus action) and attack with your action.
  3. Maul with Wild Strike Every Turn
    2 attacks per turn (3 at level 10). Use form abilities — Owlbear Crushing Flight, Myrmidon AoE, Panther Pounce.
  4. When Wild Shape drops Reactive
    You revert to gnome form with full HP and spell slots. Cast Heal, throw a big spell, or shift again. This is your second life.
When to Stay in Gnome Form

• Against many weak enemies — Call Lightning or Ice Storm clears faster
• When allies need healing — Heal (70 HP) or Healing Word
• Summoning — Conjure Animals/Elemental then let them fight
• Dialogue, puzzles, or exploration

Recommended Party

You're the flexible frontliner + off-healer. Build around sustained damage and a dedicated healer.
