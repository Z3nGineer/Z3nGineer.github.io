Our Story

How It Started

Two dudes who got tired of watching cooking shows made by people who've never paid rent.

One of us has 23 years in professional kitchens. The other one just cooks — no training, no technique books, just vibes and whatever's in the fridge. We've been cooking together for years and every single time someone says "you guys should film this."

So we finally did.

CrockedNLoaded is here to show you how to actually cook — no trust fund required. From $20 grocery runs to full-on ridiculous meals, we make real food at home and have way too much fun doing it.

The Shows

Series & Concepts

Recurring formats we're cooking up. Each one is its own kind of chaos.

01

Chef vs. Home Cook

Same dish, same ingredients, completely different approaches. Then we taste both and keep it real about who did it better.

02

$10 vs. $100

One of us gets $10, the other gets $100. Same dish. Can cheap and creative beat expensive and fancy?

03

Mystery Fridge

We stock the fridge with random ingredients and both have to make something with whatever's in there. No shopping, no excuses.

04

Crocked Classics

Taking classic comfort food recipes and putting our own spin on them. Grandma's recipe meets two guys who can't leave well enough alone.

05

Heat Check

Spice challenges, hot sauce tastings, and dishes designed to make grown men cry. Scoreboard included.

06

Kitchen Sessions

Full cook-alongs where we actually explain what we're doing and why. No jump cuts that skip the hard parts. If we screw up, you see it.

07

Broke But Hungry

$20 or less, feed yourself for a week. We've both been there — payday's a week away and the fridge is looking sad. This is how you survive.

08

No BS Reviews

Local spots, chain restaurants, whatever. We pay our own tab and say what we actually think. If it sucks, we'll tell you.
