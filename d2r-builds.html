Season 13 / Reign of the Warlock — Patch 3.1.1

Season 13 Patch 3.1.1 Warlock Class

Season 13 Notes

Reign of the Warlock

  • The Warlock arrives as D2R's first new class — an arcane-melee hybrid that conjures astral weapon projections and curses. Entirely new skill trees, items, and synergies.
  • Four new runewords — Void, Authority, Ritual, and Coven — reshape gearing for casters and melee alike.
  • Trap Assassin damage formula reworked from Patch 2.6 — traps now apply -enemy resistance, pushing Trapsin back into the meta.
  • Cold Mastery diminishing returns softened slightly: Blizzard Sorc remains a top-tier ladder starter.
  • Hammerdin unchanged — still S-tier. The cockroach of D2 builds. Will survive the heat death of the universe.
  • Lightning Sorc benefits from the new Void runeword as a Spirit alternative, though Infinity remains mandatory.

#1 Echoing Strike Warlock

#1
Warlock — New Class
Echoing Strike

The Warlock's signature build and the reason this season is named after the class. Echoing Strike creates astral weapon projections that boomerang outward, piercing through packs and returning to you for a second hit. It scales with Faster Cast Rate rather than Increased Attack Speed — making it play more like a caster than a melee class. With Mirrored Blades multiplying your projections and Blade Warp giving you teleport-like repositioning, this build shreds density at a pace that rivals Hammerdin. Community consensus: the strongest Warlock build by far.

#2 Blessed Hammer Paladin

#2
Paladin
Hammerdin

The build that has been S-tier in every single patch of Diablo 2 since 2001. Blessed Hammer deals magic damage, which almost nothing in the game is immune to. Pair it with Enigma for Teleport and you have the most consistent, versatile, and safe build in D2R. The new Void runeword is an option if you want even more FCR on your shield, but honestly Spirit is still perfect. Still S-tier. Always will be.

#3 Blizzard Sorceress

#3
Sorceress — Cold
Blizzard

The best ladder starter in Diablo 2 Resurrected, full stop. Blizzard Sorc requires zero expensive gear to function — a Spirit sword, a Lore helm, and Stealth armor will carry you through Hell. With endgame gear (Death's Fathom, Nightwing's Veil), the damage becomes absurd. You skip cold immunes rather than fight them, and Ancient Tunnels in Act 2 is your personal loot pinata with zero cold immunes. The build that funds every other build on your account.

#4 Lightning Sorceress

#4
Sorceress — Lightning
Lightning

The highest damage ceiling of any Sorceress build, and arguably the fastest clear speed in the game with endgame gear. The catch: it absolutely requires Infinity on your mercenary to break lightning immunities via Conviction aura. Without it, half the game is unkillable. With it, nothing survives. Lightning damage has a 1-to-max range, so your damage numbers will spike wildly — but the ceiling is staggeringly high. This is the build you graduate to once your Blizzard Sorc has farmed enough wealth.

#5 Lightning Sentry Assassin

#5
Assassin
Trapsin

The most laid-back build in Diablo 2. Drop your traps, walk away, collect loot. Lightning Sentry fires bolts autonomously, Death Sentry's Corpse Explosion chains through packs once the first enemy dies, and Fire Blast gives you a third damage type for stubborn immunes. Patch 2.6 buffed traps with -enemy resistance scaling, pushing this build firmly back into the meta. Three damage types (lightning, fire, physical via Corpse Explosion) means you can clear almost anything without a skip.

New Runewords — Season 13

Four new runewords introduced with Reign of the Warlock. Void and Coven are the standouts for casters; Ritual is a melee option; Authority is a Warlock leveling tool.

Runeword Runes / Base Key Stats
VoidNew Shield 40% FCR, +2 to All Skills, 15% Magic Skill Damage. Spirit replacement for Hammerdin / Warlock. The magic skill damage bonus makes it a potential upgrade for pure magic builds.
AuthorityNew Hel + Shael + Ral
Warlock Weapon		 Warlock-only leveling runeword. Cheap runes, strong early damage. The Warlock's Stealth equivalent — makes the 1-to-70 grind smooth.
RitualNew Melee Weapon 40% IAS, 260% Enhanced Attack Rating, 250% Enhanced Damage to Demons. Melee build option for demon-heavy content (Chaos Sanctuary, Pandemonium).
CovenNew Ist + Ral + Io
Helm		 +1 to All Skills, caster helm. Affordable +skills option before you find Shako. Ist is the most expensive rune — still far cheaper than Griffon's or Nightwing's.
Tip

Void is the most impactful new runeword. If you're running Hammerdin or Echoing Strike Warlock, test it against Spirit — the 15% magic skill damage may outperform Spirit's +mana and FHR depending on your other gear.
