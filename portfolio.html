OmniSuite

AI-Powered Music Production Platform

A full-stack desktop application for AI-assisted music production. OmniSuite integrates song generation, audio analysis, professional mixing and mastering, vocal production, voice conversion, image generation, and video creation into a single unified interface — all running locally on consumer hardware.

Song Generation Engine — SSE-streamed Suno integration with captcha solving, Clerk auth fallback, and 73 few-shot genre examples for prompt engineering
3-Tier Audio Analysis — Gemini AI, librosa signal processing, and offline fallback. Multi-method BPM voting, key detection, and 16-profile genre fingerprinting
Mix & Master Engine — 14 genre presets with per-stem frequency pockets, full mix bus chain, LUFS-targeted mastering. Suno WAV stem resolution with Demucs fallback
Vocal Production Pipeline — 10-stage LA studio vocal chain, RVC voice conversion with custom-trained model (6000 steps), per-layer pitch detection via pyin
Stem Separation — Demucs + UVR5 pipeline for vocal isolation, de-reverb, and de-echo. Decompose and convert lead, harmony, and ad-lib layers independently
Image Generation — Local-only pipeline: nunchaku, FLUX.1-dev, FLUX.1-schnell, and SDXL backends. No cloud dependencies
Video Pipeline — 5-tier music analysis, LLM-directed shot lists, 54 genre-specific color grades, beat-synced editorial, LTX-Video I2V animation
Lyrical AI — ChromaDB knowledge base with 452 documents, 294 artist profiles across 13 genres. Context-injected generation with universal craft principles
Instrument Lab — Full 10-stage instrument replacement pipeline with MusicGen, Stable Audio, and audio super-resolution
Chat Interface — AI assistant with SSE streaming, knowledge base retrieval, session memory, and tool orchestration across all subsystems
